Names of flowers

Number 1 : ROSE

The rose is one of the most well known flowers in the world. It is popularly known as the flower of love. Roses can be found in different colours but the red coloured rose is the most favourite one for many people. It is one of the most beautiful flowers.

Number 2 : SUNFLOWER

Sunflowers are useful plants with large flower heads, or blooms. They are named for the way they turn their blooms from east to west to follow the Sun. Sunflowers were first grown in North and South America. Today they are also grown in other parts of the world, including Europe and Asia.
